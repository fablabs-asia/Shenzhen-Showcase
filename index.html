GLOWSENSE
Author: Ancy Roshan
GLOWSENSE is an interactive flower-lamp that reacts to human presence. It “blooms” when someone approaches and glows in different colours. The mechanism combines proximity sensing, mechanical actuation, and custom lighting design.

MINUTES
Author: Abin Mathew
MINUTES is a Pomodoro productivity timer designed to help users stay focused. Built with custom electronics and fabrication, it visually guides work–break cycles to improve daily efficiency.

CHIP-E
Author: Revisankar S
CHIP-E is a friendly educational robot designed for kids. It helps young learners explore robotics through movement, interactive behaviours, and playful engagement.

MogAPad
Author: Namita Aravind
MogAPad is a multi-game electronic pad capable of running Tic-Tac-Toe, Minesweeper, and more. It blends playful design with embedded systems to create an engaging handheld gaming experience.

Lumi
Author: Noel Saji
Lumi is a character-style walking robot inspired by Toy Story. Equipped with a small TV, it reacts to people around it — playing animations when someone is nearby and walking around when no one is present.
